Grill Master Chicken Wings

Descriptions

These grilled chicken wings are always a hit! I grill them up before a party and keep them hot in a low oven. I always use Frank's RedHot sauce. It's got lots of flavor and isn't too spicy.

Ingredients

10 Serving

  • 3 pounds chicken wings
  • cut apart at joints, wing tips discarded
  • ½ cup soy sauce,
  • ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHot)
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare wings: Combine chicken wings, soy sauce, and Italian dressing in a large resealable plastic bag; coat chicken wings with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Overhead of raw chicken wings marinating in a resealable plastic bag. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Prepare sauce: Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in hot pepper sauce and soy sauce until combined. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Prepare sauce: Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir in hot pepper sauce and soy sauce until combined. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Overhead of raw chicken wings resting on a baking sheet. Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until chicken is browned and no longer pink, 25 to 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  6. Chicken wings being cooked on a grill. Place grilled wings in a large bowl. Pour butter sauce over wings; toss until well-coated.
